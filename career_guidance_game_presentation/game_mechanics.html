Game Mechanics & Algorithms

Payoff Function Algorithm

Payoff = (SkillFit × W₁) + (MarketDemand × W₂) - (RoleCompetition × W₃)
SkillFit: Player-career match
MarketDemand: Career demand
RoleCompetition: Competition
W₁, W₂, W₃: Weight parameters

Nash Equilibrium Detection

Equilibrium is detected when:

  • No player can improve payoff by switching
  • Distribution remains stable for multiple rounds

Market Dynamics Simulation

NewDemand = BaseDemand + TrendFactor - (Saturation × Impact) + RandomEvent

Career Recommendation Algorithm

RecommendationScore = (PersonalityFit × 0.4) + (SkillFit × 0.4) + (MarketOpportunity × 0.2)

Balances personal fit with market conditions for optimal recommendations.

Game Structure

The game consists of four interconnected phases that guide students through a comprehensive career exploration journey

1. Entry Phase

Personality & Skills Assessment

Players complete mini-games and scenario-based questions to assess their personality traits and skill levels. Results create a comprehensive profile used throughout the game.

2. Decision Phase

Strategic Career Selection

Players select career roles over multiple rounds, observing how their choices and the choices of others affect market conditions, competition, and opportunities.

3. Nash Equilibrium Phase

Equilibrium Visualization

The game detects and highlights when Nash Equilibrium is reachedâ€”a stable state where no player can improve their outcome by changing careers alone.

4. Reflection Phase

Personalized Recommendations

Players receive personalized career recommendations based on their profile, gameplay choices, and market conditions, along with resources for further exploration.

Each phase builds upon the previous one, creating a comprehensive learning experience about career planning and game theory principles.
